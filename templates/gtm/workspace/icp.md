# Ideal Customer Profile

## Primary ICP: Industrial B2B companies

### Firmographics
- **Revenue:** $10M - $500M+
- **Verticals:** Construction, manufacturing, distribution, wholesale, building materials
- **Geography:** US-first, expanding
- **Employees:** 50 - 5,000+

### Signals they need Balance
- Currently offering net terms manually (paper invoices, spreadsheets, aging reports in QuickBooks/SAP)
- DSO > 45 days and growing
- Credit decisions made by gut feel, not data
- Collections process is a person with a phone and a spreadsheet
- Recently raised funding or hired a CFO/Controller (signal: ready to modernize)
- Selling through a marketplace or platform that doesn't support B2B payment terms

### Buying committee
| Role | Cares about | Our pitch angle |
|---|---|---|
| CFO / VP Finance | DSO reduction, bad debt, cash flow visibility | "Cut DSO by 30%, automate credit decisions, reduce write-offs" |
| Controller / AR Manager | Daily pain of chasing invoices, manual reconciliation | "Stop chasing payments. Automate the entire AR workflow." |
| Head of Sales / CRO | Offering terms to win deals, but worried about risk | "Offer net terms to every buyer without taking on credit risk" |
| Head of Digital / IT | Integration with ERP, platform modernization | "API-first, integrates with your ERP and ecommerce stack" |
